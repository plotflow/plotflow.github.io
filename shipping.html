Domestic (U.S.)

U.S. shipping is free on every edition. Each piece ships flat in rigid, protective packaging with tracking.

  • Free standard shipping, included in the price
  • Typical delivery: 2 – 5 business days once dispatched

International

We ship to Canada, the U.K., Australia, Germany, France, and Japan for a flat $20, selected at checkout. International orders go via tracked carriers.

  • Flat $20 international shipping
  • Typical delivery: 1 – 4 weeks, depending on destination and customs
  • Customs duties and import taxes are the responsibility of the buyer

Made to Order

Every edition is plotted to order. Please allow up to 2 – 3 weeks for production before your piece ships. You will receive a tracking number as soon as your order is dispatched.

Packaging

Each piece is sleeved, layered with glassine to protect the ink, sandwiched between rigid boards, and sealed in a stay-flat mailer. We take great care to ensure your work arrives flat and in perfect condition.
