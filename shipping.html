Domestic (U.S.)

All editions ship flat in rigid, archival-safe packaging. Standard shipping within the continental United States is included in the price of every edition.

  • Standard delivery: 5 – 10 business days
  • Expedited delivery available at checkout for an additional fee

International

We ship worldwide. International orders are sent via tracked, insured carriers. Delivery times vary by destination but typically range from 10 – 21 business days.

  • International shipping fees are calculated at checkout based on destination and edition size
  • Customs duties and import taxes are the responsibility of the buyer

Made to Order

Every edition is plotted to order. Please allow 7 – 14 days for production before your piece ships. You will receive a tracking number as soon as your order is dispatched.

Packaging

Each piece is placed between acid-free tissue, backed with rigid board, and sealed in a protective sleeve before being placed in an oversize flat mailer or tube. We take great care to ensure your work arrives in perfect condition.
