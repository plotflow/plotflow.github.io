Each Universal-Century mobile suit is rebuilt as a vector drawing and traced in ink by an AxiDraw plotter on Strathmore Bristol. No printing. Every impression is unique to its edition, signed and numbered.

The Work*

Limited editions, made to order. Minor variation in line + ink is a feature of the medium.

The suit is rebuilt as a precise vector drawing. Every line mapped as a coordinate path for the plotter.

An AxiDraw traces the paths in Staedtler Triplus 0.3mm fineliner on Strathmore 300 Bristol, 11×14″.
