Custom Work

We accept a limited number of commissions for custom plotter art. If you have a mobile suit — or another subject — that you'd like drawn as a one-continuous-line piece, we'd love to discuss it.

What We Offer

  • Custom mobile suit editions — any Universal-Century suit rebuilt as a single unbroken path
  • One-of-one pieces — unique commissions that are not part of any edition run
  • Custom sizes — from A4 to A1 and beyond
  • Ink and paper options — we can work with different pen colors and paper stocks within archival-grade materials

Process

  1. Inquiry — Email us with your subject, preferred size, and any reference material
  2. Quote — We'll respond with pricing, timeline, and feasibility notes
  3. Vectorize — Once approved, we rebuild the subject as a continuous vector path
  4. Approval — You review a digital preview of the path before plotting
  5. Plot — The piece is drawn, inspected, signed, and shipped

Pricing

Commission pricing varies based on subject complexity, size, and turnaround. Custom pieces typically start at $350 for an A3 format. We'll provide a firm quote before any work begins.

Get Started

Email hello@plotflow.io with the subject line "Commission Inquiry" and tell us what you have in mind.
